# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want the Pclika HDL project to be open, practical, and welcoming to hardware designers,
FPGA developers, makers, educators, and AI tooling researchers.

Participants should help make this community respectful, constructive, and useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of different experience levels — FPGA development has a steep learning curve
- Be clear and collaborative when reviewing RTL or toolchain contributions
- Share feedback on designs in a practical and kind way
- Assume good intent while staying honest about synthesis or timing issues
- Focus on improving the platform's openness and AI-integration quality

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks on design decisions
- Discriminatory behavior
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration
- Sharing private or sensitive data without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions or participation
that violate this code.

## Reporting

Conduct or community concerns should be reported privately to the project maintainers
at **starinvc@gmail.com**.
